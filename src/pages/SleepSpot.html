Sleep Spot

Find and book a campsite - save favorites, read and write reivews.

Researching the Problem

This concept project came about in conversations with friends: finding a campground can be a frustratingly daunting process - especially for campers with "non-traditional" sleeping arrangements, such as van-camping or hammock camping. Sites that welcome these types of campers are frequently independent, and public sites are scattered between many different agency jurisdicitons. A one-stop source of site listings was a clear opportunity for exploration.

Surveying Users

To ensure that the problem was being addressed to meet real campers needs, I created and distributed a Google Survey to identify trends in camping styles, usual methods of campsite finding, and barriers to getting out. The resulting information confirmed my problem hypothesis ***.

Studying the Competition

Data from the user survey steered me towards existing apps and websites for finding campgrounds for competitive analysis.

Visualizing the User

I tied together my initial research with the creation of a user persona to keep in focus during product development.
